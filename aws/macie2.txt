NAME
       macie2 -

DESCRIPTION
       Amazon  Macie is a fully managed data security and data privacy service
       that uses machine learning and pattern matching to discover and protect
       your  sensitive data in AWS. Macie automates the discovery of sensitive
       data, such as PII and intellectual property, to provide  you  with  in-
       sight  into  the  data that your organization stores in AWS. Macie also
       provides an inventory of your Amazon S3 buckets, which  it  continually
       monitors for you. If Macie detects sensitive data or potential data ac-
       cess issues, it generates detailed findings for you to review  and  act
       upon as necessary.
